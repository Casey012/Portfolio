Process

Introduction

For this project we had to design a product that revolved around a social issue. The fridge monitor is an innovative and sustainable solution to household food waste. The fridge monitor is a system that combines user-friendly technology and environmental awareness to reduce waste, promote sustainable food consumption, and help users save money. The monitor will utilize a scanning system placed on the fridge's exterior, allowing users to track the expiration dates of stored items. An integrated recipe feature will provide meal suggestions based on available ingredients, reducing the likelihood of spoilage.

Challenges and Constraints

  1. Simplicity: this device would be used by a wide range of users, and so we needed to keep the design simple. The design had to be intuitive for users of all ages and technical knowledge.
  2. Technical Challenges: figuring out the best way to scan items and keep track of expiration dates.
  3. Placement: we wanted this device to easily fit into the routine of users rather than bypass it. So we added aditional scanners that could fit into fridges.

Prototyping

While prototyping we came up some ideas about the looks and functionality of the device. Like how big the screen should be, and we wanted to add additional sensors inside the fridge to make scanning items easier. We also thought about the UI of the screen and what that should look like.
